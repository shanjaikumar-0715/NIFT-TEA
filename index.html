Core Focus Area 01

Research & Development (R&D) Support

The CARE division focuses intensely on Research & Development concerning new apparel products, advanced manufacturing processes, and sustainable technologies to uplift Tirupur industries.

  • Development of automated prototypes like the Upgraded Mat Loom funded by DST.
  • Research on technical textiles including PBI-blended firefighter suits and carbon-based EMI shielding fabrics.
Core Focus Area 02

Industrialization of New Products & Processes

Transitioning laboratory innovations and prototypes into scalable, commercial industrial products for Tirupur MSMEs and rural weaving clusters.

  • Field deployment of upgraded automated looms for Chennimalai bedsheet weavers.
  • Commercialization support under schemes like EDII-TN Innovation Voucher Scheme (IVP).
  • Scaling sustainable dyeing processes, activated carbon wound dressings, and AI pattern generation.
Core Focus Area 03

Dissemination of New Technologies

Spreading awareness, transferring technical knowledge, and promoting modern technological standards to grassroots manufacturers and apparel clusters.

  • Organizing awareness programs, Khadi product exhibition-cum-sales stalls with KVIC.
  • Conducting student drawing competitions and promoting traditional handloom awareness.
Core Focus Area 04

Technology Upgradation Training

Equipping industrial technicians, weavers, and self-help group (SHG) members with advanced hands-on operational and technical competencies.

  • NABSKILL 30-day training for Wastewater Treatment Plant Technicians across textile units.
  • Residential CRP Coat Manufacturing Training for SHG members across Tamil Nadu.
