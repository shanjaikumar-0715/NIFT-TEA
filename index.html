Pillar 01

R&D & Prototyping Support

AIC NIFT-TEA provides robust incubation infrastructure for startups focusing on technical textiles, sustainable garments, and automated machinery prototypes.

  • Development of automated prototypes like the Upgraded Mat Loom funded by DST.
  • Research on technical textiles including PBI-blended firefighter suits and carbon-based EMI shielding.
Pillar 02

Startup Industrialization & Scaling

Helping incubatees transition their laboratory innovations and prototypes into scalable, commercial industrial products for the Tirupur apparel ecosystem.

  • Field deployment of upgraded automated looms and smart manufacturing tools.
  • Commercialization mentoring under state and central schemes like EDII-TN IVP and MSME Hackathons.
Pillar 03

Technology Dissemination & Mentorship

Spreading industry awareness, transferring technical knowledge, and connecting startup founders with veteran apparel mentors and investors.

  • Organizing startup bootcamps, investor meets, and Khadi & technical textile exhibitions.
  • Serving as a premier Knowledge Partner for institutional innovation missions.
Pillar 04

Skill Upgradation & Training

Equipping startup founders, industrial technicians, and self-help group members with advanced specialized technical competencies.

  • NABSKILL certified technician training programs for wastewater treatment and automated machinery.
  • Specialized apparel manufacturing and quality control workshops.
