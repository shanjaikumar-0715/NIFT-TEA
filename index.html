Research & Development

New Product & Process R&D Support

To strengthen the research capacity of Tirupur apparel, textile, and rural industries, CARE division provides dedicated support for formulating innovative prototypes, technical textiles, and optimized manufacturing processes.

  • Sponsored R&D projects backed by DST, NABARD, and MSME grants.
  • Advanced prototyping for technical wear and sustainable apparel.
Advanced Manufacturing

Industrialization of New Products & Processes

Transitioning successful lab-scale innovations into robust commercial manufacturing lines. We guide startups and MSMEs through NPI Studio validation, pilot testing, and full-scale industrial scaling.

  • Process optimization for machinery and automated production systems.
  • Commercialization mentoring under EDII-TN Innovation Vouchers.
Knowledge Transfer

Effective Technology Dissemination

Bridging the gap between research institutions and grassroots clusters by actively transferring cutting-edge technological standards, awareness programs, and joint industrial exhibitions directly to MSMEs.

  • Global network implementation and supply chain integration.
  • Technical documentation and standard operating procedure sharing.
Workforce Empowerment

Specialized Technology Upgradation Training

Empowering the global workforce and local artisans through specialized hands-on technical programs, residential manufacturing courses, and digital skill enhancement workshops.

  • Specialized training frameworks like NABSKILL wastewater and loom operation.
  • Real-time skill progress tracking and technical mentoring.
