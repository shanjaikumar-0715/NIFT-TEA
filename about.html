About CARE Division

Centre for Apparel Research and Education (CARE) at NIFT-TEA Knitwear Fashion Institute[cite: 1].

Empowering Tirupur Industries Through Advanced R&D

To strengthen the research capacity of Tirupur apparel, textile, and rural industries, NIFT-TEA active engagement goes beyond talent pool training. We provide comprehensive research and technology developmental support (new product/process R&D and dissemination) to MSMEs[cite: 1].

Our core objectives focus on technology upgradation training, industrialization of new processes, and fostering sustainable rural development initiatives[cite: 1].

Our Key Objectives

  • Research & Development on new products and technologies[cite: 1].
  • Industrialization of innovative manufacturing processes[cite: 1].
  • Dissemination of advanced technological standards[cite: 1].
  • Comprehensive technology upgradation training[cite: 1].
