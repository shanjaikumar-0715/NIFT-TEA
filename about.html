The Centre for Apparel Research and Education (CARE) division at NIFT-TEA Knitwear Fashion Institute acts as a premier catalyst for MSMEs. We bridge the gap between advanced academic research and grassroots industrial manufacturing.

  • Grassroots skill development for rural artisans and global workforces.
