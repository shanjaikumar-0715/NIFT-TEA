DST Scheme (₹56 Lakh Grant)

Upgraded Mat Loom for Rural Women Development

Sanctioned in September 2024 with a total budget of ₹56 lakh across a 3-year period. The project covers comprehensive Research & Development and field implementation milestones.

  • Initial prototype successfully completed and installed at AIC NIFT-TEA research centre.
  • Further manufacturing work for 5 additional looms is actively in progress to uplift rural women.
MSME Idea Hackathon 5.0 (₹12.75 Lakh Grant)

High Performance Firefighter Suit

Sanctioned ₹12.75 lakh financial support. The innovation features a PBI/Para-aramid blended fabric offering superior thermal protection, flame resistance, and extreme durability meeting international safety standards.

  • Reaffirms regional innovation potential and commitment to critical safety solutions.
NABARD DPR Project

Upgraded Power Loom for Bedsheet Weavers

Improving the livelihood level of Chennimalai power loom bedsheet weavers by upgrading conventional looms with electronic tuck-in, let-off, and take-off mechanisms.

  • Prototype upgraded loom successfully developed, tested, and utilized for bedsheet production.
  • Hands-on training provided to 18 weavers; currently in the active field deployment phase.
MSME Idea Hackathon 4.0

Advanced Industrial Protective Apparel

Building upon previous hackathon successes, this research project is currently ~50% completed. It focuses on specialized protective gear and high-durability fabrics for industrial workforces.

  • Focuses on advanced composite material integration for heavy-duty applications.
  • Rigorous thermal and tensile strength testing under progress.
EDII-TN Innovation Voucher Scheme (IVP)

Technical Textiles & AI Pattern Innovations

Under the IVP scheme, CARE department acts as a knowledge partner providing research, mentoring, and technical guidance support to innovators:

  • Miraypan Carbons (S.R. Patrick Dhanaseelan): Optimization and commercialization of carbon-based EMI shielding fabrics meeting Defence BSI standards (₹7 Lakh grant under IVP A, in progress).
  • Migara Med Fab Private Ltd (S.P. Mary Adharshna): Activated carbon fabric advanced wound dressing that achieves rapid healing (₹3 Lakh grant under IVP A, in progress).
  • Essdi Infotech Corporation (L.B. Thiyagarajan): Automatic pattern generation for textile and leather garment manufacturing using AI (₹3 Lakh grant under IVP A, in progress).
Sustainable R&D & Machinery

Eco-Friendly Dyeing & Wastewater Solutions

Addressing ecological and industrial processing challenges through cutting-edge biological, chemical, and mechanical research:

  • JM Techy (Prabhakaran P): Optimization and scaling up of sustainable dyeing process & auxiliaries for textile dyeing industries (₹3 Lakh grant under IVP A, in progress).
  • Rangaa Mannar Nuans Private Limited (K. Radha Krishnan): Optimization & scaling up of eco-friendly broad-spectrum bacterial consortium product for industrial wastewater treatment (₹3 Lakh grant under IVP A, in progress).
  • Harsa Industries (N. Krishnamurthy): Movable mechanical compactor in the spinning machine (₹3 Lakh grant under IVP A, in progress).
