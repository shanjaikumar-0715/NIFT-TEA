DST Scheme

Upgraded Mat Loom for Rural Women Development

Sanctioned in September 2024 with a total budget of ₹56 lakh across a 3-year period. The project covers Research & Development and field implementation milestones.

  • Initial prototype successfully completed and installed at AIC NIFT-TEA research centre.
  • Further manufacturing work for 5 additional looms is actively in progress.
MSME Idea Hackathon 5.0

High Performance Firefighter Suit

Sanctioned ₹12.75 lakh support. The innovation features a PBI/Para-aramid blended fabric offering superior thermal protection, flame resistance, and durability meeting international standards.

  • Reaffirms regional innovation potential and commitment to safety solutions.
NABARD DPR Project

Upgraded Power Loom for Bedsheet Weavers

Improving the livelihood level of Chennimalai power loom bedsheet weavers by upgrading conventional looms with electronic tuck-in, let-off, and take-off mechanisms.

  • Prototype upgraded loom successfully developed, tested, and used for bedsheet production.
  • Hands-on training provided to 18 weavers; currently in the field deployment phase.
MSME Idea Hackathon 4.0

Advanced Industrial Protective Apparel

Building upon previous hackathon successes, this research project is currently ~50% completed. It focuses on specialized protective gear and high-durability fabrics for industrial workforces.

  • Focuses on advanced composite material integration.
  • Rigorous thermal and tensile strength testing under progress.
EDII-TN IVP Knowledge Partner

Technical Textiles & AI Pattern Innovations

Providing research, mentoring, and technical guidance for multiple state-backed voucher projects.

  • Miraypan Carbons: Carbon-based EMI shielding fabrics meeting Defence BSI standards (₹7 Lakh grant).
  • Migara Med Fab: Activated carbon advanced wound dressings for rapid healing (₹3 Lakh grant).
  • Essdi Infotech: Automatic pattern generation for garment manufacturing using AI (₹3 Lakh grant).
Sustainable R&D

Eco-Friendly Dyeing & Wastewater Solutions

Addressing ecological and industrial processing challenges through cutting-edge biological and chemical research.

  • JM Techy: Optimization and scaling of sustainable dyeing processes & auxiliaries (₹3 Lakh grant).
  • Rangaa Mannar Nuans: Eco-friendly broad-spectrum bacterial consortium for industrial wastewater treatment (₹3 Lakh grant).
  • Harsa Industries: Movable mechanical compactor in spinning machines (₹3 Lakh grant).
