Collaborative Activities with EDII-TN

Under the Innovation Voucher Scheme (IVP), the CARE department has been providing research and technical guidance support to innovators as a knowledge partner for this scheme in the apparel and textile sector. We have also taken part in mentoring and providing market linkages to innovators.
